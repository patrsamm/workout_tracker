How Warm-ups Work

Enter the weight you plan to use for your working sets, and this app calculates your warm-up ramp automatically — straight from Jeff's guidelines:
0 warm-up sets — jump straight into working sets.

1 warm-up set — ~60% of working weight, same reps.

2 warm-up sets — ~50% (same reps), then ~70% (a few less reps).

3 warm-up sets — ~45% (same reps), ~65%, then ~85% (fewer reps each time).
Warm-up sets aren't meant to build muscle or create fatigue — they just get blood flowing and let you feel the weight before it counts. Feel free to nudge any suggested number up or down.
